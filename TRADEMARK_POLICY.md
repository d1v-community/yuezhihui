`月知会`, `FlowSense`, related logos, and associated brand identifiers are
trademarks or brand assets of the project owners and are not granted for general
use by the open-source code or content licenses in this repository.

## What is allowed

You may use the project name in factual statements such as:

- to attribute the origin of the code or content
- to describe compatibility or historical lineage
- to link to the original repository

Example:

```text
Based on the open-source Yuezhihui / FlowSense project by 月知会.
```

## What is not allowed without separate permission

- using `月知会` or `FlowSense` as the name of your own product
- using the project logo as your product or company logo
- implying endorsement, partnership, certification, or official affiliation
- presenting a fork or derivative as the official Yuezhihui / FlowSense product

## Forks and derivatives

If you build on this project, choose your own product name and visual identity.
You may keep factual attribution to the original project, but you should not
market your derivative as if it were the official 月知会 / FlowSense release.
